Delete Account Policy

1. Overview

At Memscape, we respect your right to control your data. If you decide to delete your account, all your personal information, uploaded photos, and related data will be permanently removed from our systems, in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

2. How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account by emailing us at memscape.support@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Memscape Account". Please include your registered email or UID in the message for verification.

3. What Happens After Deletion

  • All personal information, profile details, and uploaded photos will be deleted.
  • Your data will be removed from our Firestore database and authentication system.
  • This action is irreversible.

4. Need Help?

If you have any concerns or face issues with the process, feel free to contact our support team at memscape.support@gmail.com.
